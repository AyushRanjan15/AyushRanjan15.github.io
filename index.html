Hi, I’m Ayush

Welcome to my portfolio website. I hold a master’s degree in Data Science and a Bachelors degree in Electronics and Communication. Since I was a kid I was really into solving puzzles. Later I dove deep into electronics and explored the world of IOT and sensors. My IOT projects introduced me to data and I slowly started developing interest into technologies to store, manipulate and translate data into products to solve complex problems.
